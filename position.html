Position Property

Static

Element with static property can not move its positin of left/right/top/bottom

Relative

Element with Relative property can move its positin of left/right/top/bottom

Absolute Without Perent

Element with absolute position move according to body

Element move out form his normal flow

Element move with scroll

Absolute with Perent

Absolute block move according to their perent

Fixed

Element with fixed position move according to body and can not scroll

Sticky

Relative+fixed combination

Initially work as a reletive , Whenever their condition (top:10px) satisfied it work as a fixed
